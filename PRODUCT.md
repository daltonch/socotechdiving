# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recreational and technical SCUBA divers in the South County / St. Louis, MO area who
need breathing-gas fills (air, nitrox, trimix, 100% oxygen) and annual visual tank
inspections. They range from a new diver topping a single tank before a quarry trip to
CCR and cave/wreck divers prepping bailouts and deco gases. They are choosing where to
trust the most important thing they breathe underwater, often deciding by phone/text on
short notice.

## Product Purpose

A single-page marketing site for SoCo Tech Diving, an owner-run dive-gas fill station.
It exists to communicate trust and competence, list services and honest pricing, and
drive a call/text/email to book a fill or inspection. Success is a local diver
contacting the shop instead of driving to a chain dive center.

## Brand Personality

Honest, technical, unpretentious, safety-first. The voice of a working blender who dives
what he mixes: plain-spoken, precise, a little wry, never salesy. Three words:
meticulous, grounded, welcoming.

## Anti-references

- Not corporate or sterile: this is one person, not a faceless chain. No stock-smiling
  divers, no big-brand polish that erases the owner.
- Not cluttered or salesy: no hype, no popups, no badge soup, no aggressive marketing.
- Not generic-template: must not read as an off-the-shelf SaaS/landing AI generation.

## Design Principles

- Practice what you preach: the page should feel as carefully assembled as a verified
  gas blend. Craft signals competence.
- Instruments, not decoration: diving is gauges, analyzers, fill pressures. Visual
  language draws from real equipment, not generic abstraction.
- Trust through specificity: concrete numbers, real standards, honest pricing. Never
  vague reassurance.
- Welcoming expertise: technically serious without gatekeeping; a new diver and a cave
  diver both feel addressed.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Body text >= 4.5:1 contrast, large text >= 3:1. Full keyboard focus
states. Respect prefers-reduced-motion (motion is enhancement, never required to read
content). Color is never the sole carrier of meaning (gas-mix data also labeled in text).
